Li Joaquin is a famous makeup blogger in China. Recently ,he showed up several several times on the Top-Searching since he was exposure by some entertainment reporters that he smoked.
Some people think that it is unreasonable for such a famous person who is really influential to smoke. This may make the young to imitate since they are immature which means that it is especially easy for them to follow bad examples.
While to my opinion, there is nothing to complain about. It’s no strange for a adult male to smoke. Nobody else was been influenced since he didn’t smoke in public. He is not a idol or superstar, just someone who earn money by his own effort. Since he is very successful in his area and start up his own business. Many people may be jealous and feel unfair since they don’t have the ability to become entrepreneurs.

The chance for everyone on the world to success is equal. The outward ease of them always cover the huge psychological pressure they are experiencing in heart, which is closely related to the pressure of public opinion.
