RECENRLY, I haven’t come up with something exciting to record… I’m going to review the NUMT winter tournament…(mock trial competition..)

This competition was hold in january 2020, so I may be a little bit oblivious about the process about it’s sophisticated rules. During the reviewing , holpfully I can help myself remember more about it. Also someone who read this blog may know something useful about mock trial.

Before the opeaning statment , we listen to the judge states the rules. For each tournament the requirement may be different. Like whether you are required to ask for permission when approaching the witnesses or judge.

Then there will be an opeaning statement for about five mins which briefly introduce the case and your co-council. Of ourse the attorney should make the story be partical to his team even deflect the truth to some degree. (comprehensive thinking is needed just in case your rival will object which will plus for them as long as it’s accept by the judge)

Attorney is not be supposed to be so emotional during the opeaning statment. Here will be extremely challenging to your conclusion ability

The DIRECT may procceed after geting the permission from the judge. You may ask you witness in forms of leading questions to show the case in the aspect which is bias to your part no matter plaintiff or defendant you belong to.And oppositely the CROSS the attorney will oppress the opposite witness as much as possible. Meantime , the co-council of attorney may IMPEACH if the witness lies or someting else. Vise versa the OBJECTION exists. Different toranment always accompanies with different types of OBJESTION such as relevance , lack of personal knowledge, speculation(lay witness) and so on . So it’s a great challenge to the attorney’s logicality of thinking.

The closing statment is coming eventually. The closing statement is the only document that needed to be writen during the course which decied whether your team can turn the tables or not.
